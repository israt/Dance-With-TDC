WE ARE TERPSICHORE DANCE COMPANY

ABOUT [ TERPSICHORE DANCE COMPANY ]

Terpsichore Dance Company is a dance studio located in Turramurra in the North Shore area of NSW. We cater our Turramurra dance classes in several styles to all ages and ability levels. Our focus is teaching dance techniques, dance performance, safe dance, dance history and nurturing the love of dance in all of our students.

Our objective is to provide high quality dance education in a warm and friendly environment.
